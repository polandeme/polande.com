Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2024

Twitter Statistics (referred to as "we," "our," or "this extension") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

The Twitter Statistics extension only collects and stores data locally (on your computer or device). We do not upload any user data to remote servers or perform external storage. The primary data we collect includes:

  • Twitter Usage Records: We record each instance when you open or switch to a Twitter page. This record includes the timestamp and URL of the visit.

2. How We Use This Data

The data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Statistical Analysis: We use the data stored locally to generate statistics and trends about your Twitter usage, helping you understand and manage your social media activity.
  • User Interface: Display your usage records and statistics, and support data export and deletion operations.

3. Data Storage

All collected data is stored on your local device. We do not transfer data to any external servers or third parties.

4. Data Security

We use standard data storage and encryption technologies to protect the data stored on your device. As the data is stored locally, our extension does not involve online data transmission or external access.

5. Data Access and Control

You can access, export, or clear the data stored in the extension at any time. Through the extension's user interface, you can:

  • View Statistics: Check your Twitter usage data for today, this week, and this month.
  • Export Data: Export the data as a JSON file for further analysis.
  • Clear Data: Delete all records stored in the extension.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated with changes to the extension. We will note any changes to the Privacy Policy in the update notes of the extension and update this page as necessary.
